Hot Dog/Tskheli Dzaghli

Two detectives Bidzina Tabagari and Lekso Nizharadze try to investigate murder cases connected to the influential businessmen,
politicians and members of national security.

Filming

The 4th season of the series was delayed by 7 years on February 11, 2013 for certain reasons,
so this season was called the forbidden season. The premiere of the 5th season took place in the same year,
although the director of the series Zaza Urushadze left the project,
but filming continued and the 5th season of the series was directed by Irakli Badurashvili.

The series is set primarily in Tbilisi. "Hot Dog" consists of 12 episodes in Season 1, as well as banned seasons 2 and 4.
Season 3 consists of 11 episodes, and the final season consists of 9 episodes.
