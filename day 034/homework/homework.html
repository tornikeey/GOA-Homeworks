Neymar Jr Transfer History

Santos (2009-2013): Neymar began his professional career at Santos, where he developed into a world-class player.
Barcelona (2013-2017): In 2013, he moved to Barcelona for a reported €704 million, joining a star-studded team that included Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez.
Paris Saint-Germain (2017-2023): In 2017, PSG triggered his release clause, buying him for €222 million, a record transfer fee.
Al-Hilal (2023-2025): In 2023, Neymar transferred to Al-Hilal for a reported $98.6 million.
Santos (2025-Present): He returned to Santos in January 2025 on a free transfer.
