About Facebook

What is Facebook?

Facebook is a social media platform launched in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates at Harvard University. It allows users to connect with friends and family, share photos, post updates, and much more.

Key Facts

  • Founded: February 4, 2004
  • Founder: Mark Zuckerberg
  • Headquarters: Menlo Park, California, USA

Features

Facebook includes features like News Feed, Groups, Pages, Marketplace, and Messenger.

Facebook's Mission

To give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together.

Meta

In 2021, Facebook rebranded as Meta, focusing on building the Metaverse.
